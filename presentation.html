01. The Problem Space

Active vs. Passive Financial Learning

Traditional wealth platforms educate users via passive resources (blogs, videos). Users read "How Stocks Work" but close the app without acting because real money stakes create high friction.

1
The Psychological Hurdle Savers possess capital but lack execution confidence. Fear of immediate losses stalls real trading account activations.
2
The Core Opportunity How might I help users build execution confidence in real-time and learn about stocks in a way that is active, hands-on, and risk-free?
👉 Interactive Action: Look at the iPhone screen on the right. Tap standard savings balances in the setup screen to get started.
02. Proposed Strategy

Risk-Free Simulated Sandbox

To bridge the confidence gap, this strategy proposes embedding a visually identical, risk-free practicing sandbox directly inside the native app.

1
Key Assumptions Users actually want to invest but are held back by fear; confidence gained in mock environments translates to real-world trading; KYC is pre-completed.
2
Strategic Rationale By replicating the exact production trading UI, mock trades build muscle memory, reducing navigational anxiety to zero when switching to real mode.

03. Solution Overview

Embedded Financial Education

I built a fully functional React + TypeScript prototype to explore how to integrate educational guidance directly at the point of trade execution.

1
Spotlight & Guides Anchors user attention to critical workspace zones on first launch, reducing Day-1 dashboard bounces.
2
Valuation Math & Sliders Replaces dry static figures with interactive slider bottom sheets to demystify complex multiples like P/E.
3
Milestone Checklist & Reward Gamifies practice with a checklist that unlocks a Trade Fee Waiver, driving transition to real investing.

04. Onboarding Setup

Realistic Anchoring vs. Abstract Millions

Instead of starting users with a generic "₦1 Million" mock balance, the onboarding prompts them to set a capital ceiling matched to their actual savings capacity.

1
₦10k - ₦50k Capital Prompt Tying simulated cash to reality makes returns/percentage gains feel emotionally relevant to their everyday budget.
2
Input Validation Range The simulation restricts capital inputs (₦5,000 to ₦10,000,000) to keep user expectations realistic.
👉 Interactive Action: On the phone, enter a mock amount (e.g. ₦50,000) and tap "Start Paper Portfolio" to start the sandbox.
05. Onboarding Guidance

Focus & Spotlight Onboarding

A 4-step guided spotlight tour dims the screen to focus user attention on critical workspace zones, preventing Day-1 dashboard bounces.

1
Step-by-Step Guided Tour Focuses on: 1) Practice Indicator Banner, 2) Mock Capital Card, 3) Stocks Explore FAB, and 4) Weekly Performance Report Tab.
2
Precise Alignment Spotlight coordinates are calibrated to fit the viewport frame precisely, locking background clicks during the tour.
👉 Interactive Action: Tap "Next" inside the spotlight overlay on the phone screen to advance the tour.
06. Engagement Loops

The "Practice Journey" Checklist

Simulators often become empty dashboards where users bounce. This proposal integrates an interactive milestone card that gamifies user activation.

1
Structured Checklist Tasks Guides users to: 1) Research a stock, 2) Analyze P/E ratio slider, 3) Place first mock order, 4) Track live ticks, and 5) Execute a mock sell order.
2
Perfect Visual Alignment Checkbox shapes align perfectly to the first text baseline, and CTAs are stacked inline below descriptions to eliminate horizontal squeezing.
👉 Interactive Action: Observe the aligned checklist card. Tap "Browse →" to explore NG Stocks.
07. Active Learning

Interactive Sliders & Metaphors

Traditional statistics grids present dry mathematical figures. This design replaces them with a balanced grid where metrics trigger custom bottom-sheet explanations.

1
Visual Metaphorical Sheets Metric cards explain technical jargon using pizza-slice (EPS) and shopping-mall (Market Cap) analogies to ease cognitive load.
2
Valuation Slider Math Tapping P/E Ratio opens a slider mockup. Drag Share Price or Earnings to watch the valuation multiple recalculate with live indicators.
👉 Interactive Action: On the phone details view, tap **Price (P)**, **Earnings (E)**, or **P/E Ratio** to test the bottom-sheet explainers.
08. Conversion Hook

Graduation Rewards & Conversion

The funnel transitions users from practice to real-money investing through a value-generating threshold milestone.

1
Endowment & Loss Aversion Reward Once all 5 milestones check off, the user "graduates" and is rewarded with a commission-free trading waiver. Since they worked to earn it, they are highly motivated to fund an account before it expires.
2
Comparative Returns Report The report tab stacks practice yields side-by-side with static savings returns, showing savers the opportunity cost of holding idle cash.
👉 Interactive Action: Tap **"Claim Waiver & Start Real Portfolio"** on the gold graduation card to test the real-account funding prompt!
09. Outcomes & Conclusion

Expected Outcomes & Strategic Impact

Transitioning users from passive readers to active executors converts pre-educated leads at a much lower customer acquisition cost (CAC).

1
Conversion Lift & CAC A target 15–20% increase in real stock validations, with pre-qualified leads depositing higher first-time values.
2
Reduced Support & Panics The contextual explainer sheets act as a first line of support, reducing support tickets by 30% and panic selling during downturns.
3
Conclusion The risk-free simulator bridges the chasm between savings and stock investing, driving long-term user LTV and retention.
